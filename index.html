BRASS KNUCKLE GANG

A law-abiding, FCC-respecting gang 4 amateur radio operators!!

FOUNDED: 2024
TERRITORY: 144 MHz
HQ: N. Utah
MODE: CW ONLY
The name comes from "pounding brass" – the art of using manual Morse code keys 2 send CW!! We're keepin' it REAL on 2-meter CW, where the REAL operators separate themselves from the appliance operators!!

If u can't pound brass, u can't roll with us!! 🤜📡

BKG INTRODUCTION

THE 3 BYLAWS

I

NEVER TALK ABOUT BKG

EXCEPT u should actually talk about it ALL THE TIME and bring it up 2 everyone u meet!! Spread the word. Recruit the worthy. 2m CW needs MORE operators!!!

II

THE PROPER ROGER

Members must make a thoughtful effort 2 send at least one "proper Roger" per contact (QSO). Failure 2 do so may result in EXCOMMUNICATION!! 📻

III

THE QRL FORMALITY

Always send "QRL?" (Is the frequency busy?) before taking a frequency. This is done strictly as a formality on 2-meter CW – but WE DO IT ANYWAY!! 📻

1

MAKE THE CONTACT

Complete a 2-meter CW contact (QSO) with an existing BKG member!! Watch 4 us on POTA and SOTA activations in Northern Utah!! 🏔️

2

SUBMIT UR INFO

After the contact, provide the UTC time, date, and band (2m CW) 2 the member u worked!! 📝

3

GET UR NUMBER

Receive ur official BKG membership number and join the ranks of the BRASS POUNDERS!! 🤜📡
